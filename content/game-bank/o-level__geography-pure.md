  - "Simple random sampling"

misconception: stratified-vs-quota
worked: |
  Stratified random sampling first divides the population into strata (here, age groups), then selects randomly within each stratum, in proportion to the size of the stratum.

  Quota sampling also uses groups, but the people within each group are not chosen at random.
