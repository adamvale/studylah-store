*** SOURCE CORRECTION (please note in the app's Chapter-4 theory too) ***
Experiment 1 in the source markdown states that heat loss makes the electrical specific-heat-capacity
result come out "too LOW". That is physically incorrect for this method, and I corrected it in the
lesson content. Energy accounting: you supply E = Pt and compute c = Pt / (m DeltaTheta), crediting
ALL the supplied energy to the block; but Pt = mc DeltaTheta + heat lost, so the real m c DeltaTheta
is LESS than Pt, which makes the measured c an OVERESTIMATE - it comes out TOO HIGH. The TP4 content
(p4-4 teach card, choice and open; p4-rev2 open; p4-quiz spoterror and open) all now say TOO HIGH.
Recommend the source's Experiment-1 wording ("too low") be fixed to "too high" for consistency.
(Unrelated and left as-is: in the FUSION experiment, WITHOUT the control funnel l_f comes out too
SMALL - that direction is correct and is a different experiment.)

    measured c TOO HIGH (see correction above).
