CALCULATION BANK (all independently recomputed, all match the marked answers):
  Transformer  V_s / V_p = N_s / N_p ; ideal V_p I_p = V_s I_s
   - 240 V, 1200 -> 60 turns:  V_s = 240 x 60 / 1200 = 12 V (step-down)
   - 240 V, 800 -> 200 turns:  V_s = 240 x 200 / 800 = 60 V (step-down)
   - step-down 230 V -> 46 V (5:1), N_s = 120 -> N_p = 600; I_s = 230 x 1.2 / 46 = 6.0 A
   - step-up 120 V, I_p = 0.30 A, I_s = 0.015 A:  V_s = 120 x 0.30 / 0.015 = 2400 V
  Power transmission  P_loss = I^2 R, I = P / V
   - 8 kW down 0.5 ohm at 400 V:  I = 20 A, P_loss = 20^2 x 0.5 = 200 W
   - same 8 kW at 4000 V:         I = 2 A,  P_loss = 2^2 x 0.5 = 2 W (far less lost)
